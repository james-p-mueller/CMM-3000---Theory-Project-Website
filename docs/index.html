Relational Dialectics Theory Project

Relational Dialectics Theory (RDT), is a communications theory that posits that our relationships are in an ever-changing state that is influenced by a multitude of factors. These factors might be as obvious as the partners in a relationship, but also include things like their respective careers or the area they live in.
