RDT in Friendships

Section Author - James Pollnow

“in friendships, however, people may satisfactorily juggle casual friendships and closer friendships, and many friendships may never develop deep levels of intimacy” “levels of friendships, such as casual, close, and best. Relationships are also able to oscillate between these levels and exhibit spiraling change, where tendencies recur continually but not in an identical fashion” “amount of contact was less important and affection was more important in closer friendships. Casual friendships were more likely to end due to lack of proximity, whereas close and best friendships were more likely to end due to decreased affection.”
