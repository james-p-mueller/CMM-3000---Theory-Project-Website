RDT in Romantic Relationships

Section Author - James Mueller

RDT has a large role in romantic relationships, and many couples would benefit from having a better understanding from some of the central concepts related to RDT.

For example, it is extremely important for people in a relationship to be communicating properly with each other in order to make sure that the other person is still happy and satisfied. Knowing to be open about your feelings with your partner is an extremely valuable skill. This is tied to openness and closedness.

In the context of sameness versus change, many couples have reported succesful rekindlings of stale relationships after introducing a new element to their relationship, whether it be making extra time for your partner, or spicing things up.
